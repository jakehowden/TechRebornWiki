# Bronze Tools & Armor

**Bronze** tools and armor provide an early-game alternative to iron, requiring [Bronze Ingots](/docs/materials/ingots/bronze_ingot) crafted in the Alloy Smelter or Industrial Blast Furnace. Bronze gear is slightly stronger than iron in most respects.
