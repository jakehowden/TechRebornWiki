# Industrial Grinder

<MachineStats power={128} buffer={10000} tier="Medium" inputSlots={2} outputSlots={4} />

## Overview
The Industrial Grinder is a significant upgrade over the basic Grinder. By introducing a fluid (like Water or Sodium Persulfate) during the grinding process, it massively increases your ore yields and can produce secondary byproduct dusts.

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot, 1 Fluid Input
- **Output:** Up to 4 Item Slots for primary dusts and secondary byproducts.
- **Power:** 128 E/t (from Medium Voltage)
