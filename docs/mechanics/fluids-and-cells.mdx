# Fluids & Cells

Tech Reborn introduces many new fluids used in processing. You can move fluids around using pipes from other mods, or by using Cells.

## Empty Cells

Cells are the standard way to store and transport fluids manually in Tech Reborn. 

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/cell" />

You can place an Empty Cell into a machine's fluid slot to extract its fluid, or place a filled cell into the slot to insert the fluid.

## Tank Units

For bulk fluid storage, you can build Tank Units. These act as large, single-block fluid tanks. They come in various tiers with increasing capacities.

## Common Fluids

Many advanced recipes require or produce fluids like Lithium, Hydrogen, Compressed Air, or Oil. *(For a full list of fluids and their uses, see the [Fluids](../world/fluids) page).*
