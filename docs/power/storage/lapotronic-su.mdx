## Overview
The Lapotronic SU (LSU) is a multiblock storage structure. By surrounding the controller with LSU Storage blocks, you can expand its capacity almost infinitely.

## Behavior
- Input: Insane Voltage (IV).
- Output: Insane Voltage (IV).
- Special: The capacity increases significantly for each LSU Storage block placed adjacent to it.
- Configurable values: `lesuStoragePerBlock`, `lesuExtraIOPerBlock`, `lesuBaseOutput`
