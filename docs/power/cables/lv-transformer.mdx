## Overview
The LV Transformer is used to safely transfer energy between a Medium Voltage (MV) network and a Low Voltage (LV) network.

## Behavior
- **Default Mode (Step Down):** Accepts up to 128 E/t (MV) into the dotted face, and outputs up to 32 E/t (LV) from all other faces.
- **Inverted Mode (Step Up):** When inverted with a Wrench or Redstone signal, it accepts LV into any face and outputs MV out of the dotted face.
