**Resin Basin** is a non-electric passive block in Tech Reborn. When attached to a rubber log, it slowly collects Raw Rubber sap without requiring any player interaction.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/resin_basin" />

## Behavior

- Must be placed on the side of a Rubber Tree log (the log must have a resin hole texture on that face).
- Produces Raw Rubber passively at a configurable rate (default: configurable via `techreborn.json` under `resin_basin.saptime`).
- Right-clicking an attached basin collects the raw rubber if any has accumulated.
- No energy consumption — purely time-based.
