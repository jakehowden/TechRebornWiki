## Overview
The Grinder breaks down ores and other materials into Dusts. Grinding ores instead of directly smelting them generally doubles your ingot yield.[^1]

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot
- **Output:** 1 Item Slot
- **Power:** 32 E/t (from Low Voltage)
