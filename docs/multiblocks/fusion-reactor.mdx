## Overview
The Fusion Reactor is an end-game multiblock used for generating extreme amounts of power or synthesizing rare elements. It requires a massive ring of Fusion Coils to operate, and the size of the ring determines its maximum power and crafting speed.

## Multiblock Structure
*The Fusion Reactor is built as a large hollow torus (ring) of Fusion Coils centered around the Control Computer. The ring radius is configurable via the GUI (default size 6). Below is an illustrative placeholder.*
