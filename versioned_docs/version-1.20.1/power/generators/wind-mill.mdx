## Overview
The Wind Mill generates power depending on its height in the world and the current weather. Thunderstorms increase generation significantly.

## Behavior
- Input: None (passive).
- Output: Energy (E). Scales with Y-level up to Y=255.
- Configurable values: `windMillMaxOutput`, `windMillMaxEnergy`, `windMillBaseEnergy`, `windMillThunderMultiplier`
