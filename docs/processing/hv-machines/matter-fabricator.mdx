## Overview
The Matter Fabricator is a pinnacle end-game machine. It consumes vast quantities of power to slowly generate UU-Matter. Inserting Scrap or Scrap Boxes drastically speeds up the process and reduces the energy cost per UU-Matter.

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot (Scrap / Scrap Boxes, optional but highly recommended)
- **Output:** Fluid (UU-Matter)
- **Power:** 8192 E/t (Insane Voltage)

## Recipes accepted
*The Matter Fabricator passively generates UU-Matter and has no standard processing recipes.*
