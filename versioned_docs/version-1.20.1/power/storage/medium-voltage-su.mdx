## Overview
The Medium Voltage SU (Storage Unit), formerly known as the MFE, is the second tier of energy storage. It holds 4,000,000 E and handles a transfer rate of 128 E/t.

## Behavior
- Input: Receives energy up to 128 E/t.
- Output: Emits energy up to 128 E/t.
- Special: Can charge MV and LV batteries placed in its item slots.
