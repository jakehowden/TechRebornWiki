# Circuits

Circuits are the backbone of almost all advanced machinery in Tech Reborn. You will need to automate their production early on.

## Electronic Circuit

The basic Electronic Circuit is used in almost all Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) machinery.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/electronic_circuit" />

**Consumed By:**
- Assembling Machine (e.g., to create Data Storage Cores)
- Various crafting recipes for machines and tools.

## Advanced Circuit

The Advanced Circuit is required for High Voltage (HV) machines and complex components.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/advanced_circuit" />

**Consumed By:**
- Assembling Machine
- High-tier machinery and multiblocks.
