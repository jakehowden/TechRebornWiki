## Overview
The Industrial Electrolyzer is a core Medium Voltage (MV) machine that breaks materials down into their constituent elements. For instance, it can process Water into Hydrogen and Oxygen cells.

## Behavior
- **Input:** Up to 2 Item Slots, capable of taking fluids from cells.
- **Output:** Up to 4 Item Slots for various byproducts.
- **Power:** 128 E/t (from Medium Voltage)
