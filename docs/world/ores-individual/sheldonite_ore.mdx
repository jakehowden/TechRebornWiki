# Sheldonite Ore

**Sheldonite Ore** is a Tech Reborn ore block found in the End. Mine and process via the Industrial Grinder (boosted yield) or Furnace.

## How to obtain

Found in The End during world generation. Mine with a pickaxe to obtain the ore drop, or use Silk Touch to collect the block itself.
