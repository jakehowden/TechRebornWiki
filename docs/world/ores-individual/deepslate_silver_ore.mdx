# Deepslate Silver Ore

**Deepslate Silver Ore** is a Tech Reborn ore block found in the Overworld deepslate. Mine and process via the Industrial Grinder (boosted yield) or Furnace.

## How to obtain

Found in deep Overworld layers (deepslate zone, typically below Y 0) during world generation. Mine with a pickaxe to obtain the ore drop, or use Silk Touch to collect the block itself.

## Used in

_This item is not a direct ingredient in any extracted recipes._
