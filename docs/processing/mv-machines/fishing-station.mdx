**Fishing Station** is a Medium-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. It automatically performs fishing operations and deposits the results into its output inventory.

## Behavior

- Catches a fish (or other fishing loot) every 400 ticks (20 seconds) by default.[^1]
- Consumes 500 E per catch.[^1]
- Must have water adjacent to function.
- Output goes into the machine's output slots.
- Loot table is the same as vanilla fishing — includes fish, junk, and treasure.
