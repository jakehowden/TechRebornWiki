## Overview
The Water Mill generates energy passively when placed adjacent to Water blocks. It is a slow but infinite source of early-game Low Voltage (LV) energy.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/water_mill" />

## Behavior
- Input: None (passive).
- Output: Energy based on surrounding water blocks.
- Configurable values: `waterMillMaxOutput`, `waterMillMaxEnergy`, `waterMillEnergyMultiplier`
