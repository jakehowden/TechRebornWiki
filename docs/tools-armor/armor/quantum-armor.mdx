# Quantum Armor

Quantum Armor is the pinnacle of Tech Reborn protection. Crafted from Iridium Alloy Plates and Nano Armor, it renders the wearer practically invincible as long as it has power. Each piece provides unique abilities like night vision, flight, speed, and fall damage negation.

Quantum Armor has a massive energy capacity and must be charged in High Voltage (HV) or higher storage units.
