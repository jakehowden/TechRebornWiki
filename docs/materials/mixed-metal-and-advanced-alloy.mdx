# Advanced Alloy Chain

Advanced Alloy Plates and Ingots are critical for High Voltage and advanced tech tiers.

## 1. Mixed Metal Ingot
The foundation of advanced alloys is the Mixed Metal Ingot. This requires combining Bronze, Refined Iron, and Tin plates in a crafting table.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/mixed_metal_ingot" />

## 2. Advanced Alloy Ingot
Currently, the mixed metal ingot is typically compressed directly, but in some versions, you may smelt it into an Advanced Alloy Ingot first. Check standard recipes or simply compress the Mixed Metal Ingot.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/advanced_alloy_ingot" />

## 3. Advanced Alloy Plate
Compressing the Mixed Metal Ingot or Advanced Alloy Ingot in a Compressor produces the final Advanced Alloy Plate.
