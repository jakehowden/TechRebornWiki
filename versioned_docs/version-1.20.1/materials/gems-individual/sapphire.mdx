# Sapphire

**Sapphire** is a gemstone obtained from [Sapphire Ore](../../world/ores-individual/sapphire_ore) found in the Overworld. Process the ore in the Industrial Grinder for additional yield.

## How to obtain

Mine [Sapphire Ore](../../world/ores-individual/sapphire_ore) in the Overworld. The ore drops Sapphire when mined without Silk Touch. Use Silk Touch to collect the ore block and process it in the Industrial Grinder for boosted output.
