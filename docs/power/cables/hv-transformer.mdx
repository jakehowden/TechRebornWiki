## Overview
The HV Transformer is used to safely transfer energy between an Extreme Voltage (EV) network and a High Voltage (HV) network.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/hv_transformer" />

## Behavior
- **Default Mode (Step Down):** Accepts up to 2048 E/t (EV) into the dotted face, and outputs up to 512 E/t (HV) from all other faces.
- **Inverted Mode (Step Up):** When inverted with a Wrench or Redstone signal, it accepts HV into any face and outputs EV out of the dotted face.
