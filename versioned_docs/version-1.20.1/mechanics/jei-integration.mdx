# JEI Integration

Tech Reborn relies heavily on **Just Enough Items (JEI)** (or its equivalents like REI / EMI). 

This wiki is an overview of the mod's mechanics, but **JEI is your in-game source of truth for all recipes**.

## Keybinds

When hovering over an item in your inventory or the JEI list:
- Press **`R`** (Recipe) to see how to craft the item.
- Press **`U`** (Uses) to see what the item can be used to craft.

## Multiblocks in JEI

For multiblock machines (like the Industrial Blast Furnace), you can view their required structure in JEI. Open the machine's recipes and look for the **"Show Structure"** or similar icon. This will help you build the structure layer-by-layer in the world.
