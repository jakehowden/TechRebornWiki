# Sphalerite Ore

**Sphalerite Ore** is a Tech Reborn ore block found in the Nether. Mine and process via the Industrial Grinder (boosted yield) or Furnace.

## How to obtain

Found in the Nether during world generation. Mine with a pickaxe to obtain the ore drop, or use Silk Touch to collect the block itself.
