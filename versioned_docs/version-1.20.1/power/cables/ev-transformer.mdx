## Overview
The EV Transformer is used to safely transfer energy between an Insane Voltage (IV) network and an Extreme Voltage (EV) network.

## Behavior
- **Default Mode (Step Down):** Accepts up to 8192 E/t (IV) into the dotted face, and outputs up to 2048 E/t (EV) from all other faces.
- **Inverted Mode (Step Up):** When inverted, it accepts EV into any face and outputs IV out of the dotted face.
