# Road to the Quarry

This guide walks you from a brand new world to your first automated ore miner: the **Quarry** from the [Quarry Reborn](https://modrinth.com/mod/quarry-reborn) mod. The quarry mines ores in a 7-block radius (configurable) and consumes 6,000 EU per block mined, making it a powerful late-game automation tool.

The quarry is a significant undertaking — it touches nearly every tier of Tech Reborn. The guide is broken into clear milestones you can pause between.

---

## 1. LV Foundation

Before anything else you need a working Low Voltage setup. If you haven't done this yet, complete the [Getting Started guide](./getting-started) first.

When you're done you should have all of the following running:

| Machine | Why you need it |
|---|---|
| Solid Fuel Generator | Power source |
| Grinder | Produces dusts |
| Compressor | Produces plates |
| Alloy Smelter | Produces Bronze and other alloys |
| Electric Furnace | Faster smelting |
| **Extractor ×2** | **Goes directly into the quarry — build two** |

Build the two Extractors now even if you only need one operationally. They are direct ingredients in the final quarry recipe.

## 5. Rolling Machine and Heating Coils

The Industrial Blast Furnace requires **Cupronickel Heating Coils** in its crafting recipe. These are made in the Rolling Machine, which is a powered crafting table that runs on 32 E/t.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/rolling_machine" />

Once built, use JEI or REI in-game to look up the Cupronickel Heating Coil recipe — it requires Cupronickel plates shaped in the Rolling Machine. You will need four coils to craft the IBF.

## 6. Industrial Blast Furnace

With Advanced Machine Frames, Advanced Circuits, Cupronickel Heating Coils, and Electric Furnaces ready, you can now craft the IBF controller block and assemble the multiblock.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/industrial_blast_furnace" />

The IBF is a multiblock that must be assembled in the world. See the [Industrial Blast Furnace](../multiblocks/industrial-blast-furnace) page for the full layer layout.

**Power:** The IBF requires **128 E/t (Medium Voltage)**. You will need an MV power source or a step-up transformer fed by multiple LV generators. See [Power Tiers](../mechanics/power-tiers) for options.

**Heat tiers** determine which recipes the IBF can run:

| Heat | What it unlocks |
|---|---|
| 1,000 | Steel Ingot |
| 1,500 | Titanium Ingot |
| 1,700 | Aluminum Ingot |
| 2,000 | Iridium Ingot |

Heat is increased by upgrading the heating coils in the multiblock structure (Kanthal → Nichrome). You do not need heat 2,000 yet — start at 1,000 and upgrade later.

## 13. Using the Quarry

Place the quarry in the world, connect at least **MV-tier power**, and it will mine a 7×7 column of ores beneath it by default.

- **Power:** 6,000 EU per block mined (128 E/t draw while running).
- **Tech Reborn Overclocker Upgrades** increase speed at the cost of higher power draw.
- **Quarry Reborn Range Extender Upgrades** increase the mining radius up to 24 blocks.
- Enable **Excavation Mode** in the quarry's GUI to mine all blocks, not just ores.
