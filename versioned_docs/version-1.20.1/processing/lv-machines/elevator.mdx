**Elevator** is a Low-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. When multiple Elevator blocks are stacked vertically, players can teleport between them by pressing the jump or sneak keys.

## Behavior

- Press **Jump** while standing on an Elevator to teleport up to the next Elevator in the column.
- Press **Sneak** while standing on an Elevator to teleport down to the next Elevator below.
- Costs 2 E per block of vertical travel (configurable in `techreborn.json`).
- Can optionally travel through solid blocks if `AllowElevatingThroughBlocks` is enabled in config.
- Elevator blocks must be in the same X/Z column to link.
