## Overview
The Creative Solar Panel provides effectively infinite energy.[^1] It is not craftable — obtain it via the creative inventory or `/give`.

## Behavior
- Input: None.
- Output: Unlimited Energy (E).

[^1]: Output is `Integer.MAX_VALUE` E/t (~2.1 billion). No recipe exists; the item cannot be obtained in survival mode.
