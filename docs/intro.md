# Tech Reborn Wiki

An unofficial reference for the [Tech Reborn](https://github.com/TechReborn/TechReborn) Minecraft mod — Minecraft 1.20.1 edition.

Tech Reborn is a technology mod focused on power generation, ore processing, and automation. It covers hundreds of items, blocks, and machines across multiple power tiers (LV, MV, HV, EV, IV).

## What's here

- **Power** — generators, storage units, cables, and transformers
- **Processing** — every machine from the Compressor to the Industrial Blast Furnace
- **Tools & Armor** — drills, chainsaws, jetpacks, nano/quantum sets
- **Materials** — ingots, plates, dusts, cells, and miscellaneous components
- **Storage Units & Tanks** — Batbox through MFSU, fluid tanks
- **Multiblocks** — Industrial Blast Furnace, Fusion Reactor, and more
- **World** — ores, rubber trees, fluids
- **Mechanics** — power tiers, voltage rules, cable loss
- **Guides** — beginner progression and walkthroughs

## Notes

- All stats are **default values** — servers can configure most values via `TechRebornConfig`.
- Recipes are verified against the 1.20.1 mod source. If something looks wrong, open an issue.
- Quarry Reborn coverage is out of scope for this wiki.
