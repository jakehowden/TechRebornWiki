# Industrial Chunk Loader

**Industrial Chunk Loader** is a utility block in Tech Reborn. Once placed, it forces the surrounding chunks to remain loaded, keeping machines and processes running even when no player is nearby.

## Recipe

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/chunk_loader" />

## Behavior

- Keeps chunks in a configurable radius loaded (default radius: 5, configurable in `techreborn.json`).
- No energy consumption — always active while placed.
- Right-clicking opens a GUI showing the loaded chunk map.
- The loaded area is tied to the block's position; breaking the block releases the chunks.
