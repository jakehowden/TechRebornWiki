# Iron Furnace

## Overview
The Iron Furnace is an early-game alternative to the standard Cobblestone Furnace. It smelts items slightly faster and is more fuel efficient, but it still runs on solid fuel (Coal, Wood, etc.) rather than electricity.

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot, 1 Fuel Slot
- **Output:** 1 Item Slot
- **Power:** None (Requires Solid Fuel)

## Recipes accepted
*The Iron Furnace accepts all standard Minecraft smelting recipes.*
