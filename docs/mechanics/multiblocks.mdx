# Multiblocks

Some of the most powerful machines in Tech Reborn are **Multiblocks**. A multiblock is a machine made of multiple blocks arranged in a specific pattern.

## The Controller

Every multiblock has a central **Controller** block. This is the block you interact with to insert items, provide power, and retrieve outputs. The other blocks that make up the structure are usually Casings.

> **Tip:** Use JEI! If you click on the Controller block in JEI, you can click the "Show Structure" button to see a holographic overlay of how to build the multiblock in the world.

## Machine Casings

The tier of your multiblock often depends on the type of Machine Casing used in its structure. There are three tiers of casings:
- **Basic Machine Casing**
- **Advanced Machine Casing**
- **Industrial Machine Casing**

## Heat Mechanics (Industrial Blast Furnace)

The Industrial Blast Furnace (IBF) uses a unique heat mechanic. Its recipes require a minimum heat value to process. 

You can increase the heat of your IBF by:
1. Using higher tier casings (e.g., Advanced or Industrial instead of Basic).
2. Placing Lava or other hot fluids in the center column of the multiblock.

Better casings and more lava = more heat = more advanced recipes unlocked.

*(Specific multiblock structures like the IBF and Industrial Grinder are each covered on their dedicated multiblock pages.)*
