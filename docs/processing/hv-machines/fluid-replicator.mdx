## Overview
The Fluid Replicator is a late-game machine that consumes UU-Matter and massive amounts of energy to artificially create fluids.

## Behavior
- **Input:** UU-Matter.
- **Output:** Selected fluids.
- **Power:** 256 E/t (High Voltage)
- **Special:** Requires data patterns or configurations to select the output fluid.

## Recipes accepted
*Fluid Replicator recipes often rely on fluid registry configurations rather than standard item recipes.*
