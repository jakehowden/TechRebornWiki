## Overview
The Recycler destroys unwanted items, giving a small chance to produce Scrap. Scrap can be crafted into Scrap Boxes, which provide random items when opened or can be processed for other uses.

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot (accepts almost any block or item).
- **Output:** 1 Item Slot (Scrap, generated probabilistically).
- **Power:** 32 E/t (from Low Voltage)
- **Configurable values:** `recyclerChance`

## Recipes accepted
*The Recycler accepts virtually any item and has a chance to turn it into Scrap.*
