# Dragon Egg Syphon

<MachineStats power={128} buffer={1000} tier="Medium" />

## Overview
The Dragon Egg Syphon passively generates Medium Voltage (MV) energy when a Dragon Egg is placed on top of it. It requires no fuel, making it a very valuable mid-game passive generator.

## Recipe
*Check JEI for the exact recipe if enabled in your modpack.*

## Behavior
- Input: Adjacent Dragon Egg.
- Output: Energy (E).
