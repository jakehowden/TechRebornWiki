**Charge-O-Mat** is a High-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. It charges or discharges energy-storing items (batteries, tools, armor) placed in its inventory slots.

## Behavior

- Input and output rate: 512 E/t (High Voltage).
- Internal buffer: 100,000,000 E (configurable).
- Charges any Tech Reborn energy item placed in its inventory slots.
- Can also discharge items (drain them back into the machine's buffer).
- Multiple item slots allow charging several items simultaneously.

## Notes

The 100 million E buffer is intentionally very large — it can act as a secondary energy storage while serving as a charging station.
