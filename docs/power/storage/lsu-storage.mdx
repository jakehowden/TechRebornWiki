**LSU Storage** is an energy storage expansion block in Tech Reborn. It is placed adjacent to a [Lapotronic SU](./lapotronic-su.mdx) to increase its total energy capacity.

## Recipe

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/lsu_storage" />

## Behavior

- Does not function as a standalone storage block.
- Each LSU Storage block placed adjacent to (touching) a Lapotronic SU increases the Lapotronic SU's capacity.
- The Lapotronic SU scans for adjacent LSU Storage blocks and accumulates their capacity contribution.
