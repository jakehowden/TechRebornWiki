**Fusion Coil** is a multiblock component in Tech Reborn. It is used to construct the ring of the Fusion Reactor, which is the highest-tier power and processing structure.

## Recipe

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/fusion_coil" />

## Behavior

- Does not function as a standalone block.
- 32 Fusion Coils are required to form the ring surrounding the Fusion Control Computer.
- Coils must be placed in a specific pattern around the Fusion Control Computer — refer to the Fusion Reactor multiblock guide.
