# Sapphire Ore

**Sapphire Ore** is a Tech Reborn ore block found in the Overworld. Mine and process via the Industrial Grinder (boosted yield) or Furnace.

## How to obtain

Found in Overworld stone layers during world generation. Mine with a pickaxe to obtain the ore drop, or use Silk Touch to collect the block itself.
