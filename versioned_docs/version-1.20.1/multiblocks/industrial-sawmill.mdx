## Overview
The Industrial Sawmill slices logs into planks with extremely high efficiency. When supplied with a liquid like Water, it produces significantly more planks per log than manual crafting, alongside byproducts like Sawdust.

## Behavior

- **Output:** Up to 3 Item Slots (Planks + Sawdust)
- **Power:** 128 E/t (Medium Voltage default)
