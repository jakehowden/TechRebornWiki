## Overview
The Low Voltage SU (Storage Unit), formerly known as the BatBox, is your first tier of energy storage. It can store up to 40,000 E and output at a rate of 32 E/t.

## Behavior
- Input: Receives energy up to 32 E/t.
- Output: Emits energy up to 32 E/t.
- Special: Wrenching it allows you to rotate the output face.
