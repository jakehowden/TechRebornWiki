## Overview
The Plasma Generator is a late-game Extreme Voltage (EV) generator that uses Heliumplasma or other plasmas. It produces an incredibly high amount of energy.

## Behavior
- Input: Plasma fluids.

- Configurable values: `plasmaGeneratorMaxOutput`, `plasmaGeneratorMaxEnergy`, `plasmaGeneratorEnergyPerTick`
