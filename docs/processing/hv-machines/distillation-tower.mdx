## Overview
The Distillation Tower processes raw fluids into advanced fuels and chemicals. For instance, pumping Oil into the tower yields Diesel, Sulfuric Acid, and other byproducts.

## Behavior
- **Input:** Fluid input or cells.
- **Output:** Up to 4 Item Slots for byproduct cells/items, plus fluid outputs.
- **Power:** 128 E/t (Medium Voltage default)
