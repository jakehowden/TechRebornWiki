# UU-Matter

**UU-Matter** is a Matter Fabricator output used as a universal crafting material.

## How to obtain

Produced by the Matter Fabricator, a high-tier machine that converts EU into matter. UU-Matter can be crafted into many Tech Reborn items as a substitute material.
