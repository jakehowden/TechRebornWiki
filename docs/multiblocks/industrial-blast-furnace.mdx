<MachineStats power={128} buffer={10000} tier="Medium" inputSlots={2} outputSlots={2} />

## Overview
The Industrial Blast Furnace (IBF) is a critical mid-to-late game multiblock. It is used to smelt advanced materials like Steel, Titanium, and Tungstensteel. The recipes it accepts depend on its maximum heat, which is determined by the tier of machine casings and the presence of lava used in its structure.
