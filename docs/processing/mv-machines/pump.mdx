**Pump** is a Medium-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. It scans for and collects fluid source blocks, storing the resulting fluid in its internal tank.

## Behavior

- Collects one fluid source block per operation, consuming 1,000 E per block by default.[^1]
- Each operation takes 100 ticks (5 s) by default.[^1]
- Scan direction: inward-to-outward by default.[^1]
- Fluid is stored in the machine's internal tank; output via fluid pipes or by connecting buckets.
- Can pump lava, water, and other fluid sources.
