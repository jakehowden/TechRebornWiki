## Overview
The Lightning Rod does not generate energy passively. Instead, it acts as a target for lightning strikes during thunderstorms. When struck, it instantly generates a massive burst of Insane Voltage (IV) energy.

## Behavior
- Input: Lightning strikes.
- Output: Burst Energy (E).
- Special: Requires robust storage capable of accepting a sudden, massive influx of power without exploding.
