# Electric Wrench

The **Electric Wrench** is an energy-powered variant of the [Wrench](./wrench) that safely removes Tech Reborn machines without losing them. It draws power from its internal battery rather than using durability.

## Charging

The Electric Wrench can be charged in any Low Voltage (LV) or higher energy storage unit.
