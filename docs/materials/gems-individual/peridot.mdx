# Peridot

**Peridot** is a gemstone obtained from [Peridot Ore](../../world/ores-individual/peridot_ore) found in The End. Process the ore in the Industrial Grinder for additional yield.

## How to obtain

Mine [Peridot Ore](../../world/ores-individual/peridot_ore) in The End. The ore drops Peridot when mined without Silk Touch. Use Silk Touch to collect the ore block and process it in the Industrial Grinder for boosted output.
