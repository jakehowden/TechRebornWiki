# Solid Fuel Generator

<MachineStats power={32} buffer={10000} tier="Low" />

## Overview
The Solid Fuel Generator is typically the first power source you build. It burns solid items like Coal, Wood, and Charcoal to generate Low Voltage (LV) energy.

- Output: Energy (E).
