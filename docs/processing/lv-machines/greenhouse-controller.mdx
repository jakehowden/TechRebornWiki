**Greenhouse Controller** is a Low-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. It scans crop blocks in its operating range, applies bonemeal to accelerate growth, and harvests mature crops automatically.

## Behavior

- Idle consumption: 2 E/t (configurable).
- Energy per harvest: 100 E (configurable).
- Energy per bonemeal application: 50 E (configurable).
- Operates on the chunk the controller is in and adjacent loaded chunks within range.
- Requires bonemeal in its inventory to use the bonemeal-growth feature.
- Harvested items are deposited into the machine's output inventory or dropped nearby.
