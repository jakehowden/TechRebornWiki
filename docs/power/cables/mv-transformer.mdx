## Overview
The MV Transformer is used to safely transfer energy between a High Voltage (HV) network and a Medium Voltage (MV) network.

## Behavior
- **Default Mode (Step Down):** Accepts up to 512 E/t (HV) into the dotted face, and outputs up to 128 E/t (MV) from all other faces.
- **Inverted Mode (Step Up):** When inverted with a Wrench or Redstone signal, it accepts MV into any face and outputs HV out of the dotted face.
