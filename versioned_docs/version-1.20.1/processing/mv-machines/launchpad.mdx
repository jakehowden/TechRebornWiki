**Launchpad** is a Medium-Voltage utility machine in Tech Reborn. Entities standing on it are launched upward with a configurable speed when the machine has sufficient energy.

## Behavior

- Four speed levels selectable via the GUI (configurable in `techreborn.json`):

| Level | Default Speed | Energy Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Low | 1.0 | 1,000 E |
| Medium | 3.0 | 6,000 E |
| High | 5.0 | 10,000 E |
| Extreme | 10.0 | 20,000 E |

- Default selection at placement: Low (index 0).
- Launch interval: 100 ticks (5 s) between successive launches (configurable).
- Works on players and mobs alike.
