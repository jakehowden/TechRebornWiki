## Overview
The High Voltage SU (Storage Unit), formerly known as the MFSU, is the third tier of energy storage. It holds an immense 40,000,000 E and supports a transfer rate of 512 E/t.

## Behavior
- Input: Receives energy up to 512 E/t.
- Output: Emits energy up to 512 E/t.
- Special: Capable of charging HV items like the Lapotron Crystal.
