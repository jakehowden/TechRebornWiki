## Overview
The Auto Crafting Table runs on electricity to automatically craft standard 3x3 Minecraft crafting recipes continuously as long as it is supplied with ingredients.

## Recipe
<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/auto_crafting_table" />

## Behavior
- **Input:** Inventory for ingredients.
- **Output:** Crafted items.
- **Power:** 32 E/t (from Low Voltage)
- **Special:** Configure the desired recipe in its UI grid.

## Recipes accepted
*Accepts standard vanilla and modded 3x3 crafting recipes.*
