# Iridium Alloy

Iridium Alloy is an incredibly strong material used in Quantum armor, matter fabricators, and advanced energy storage. 

## 1. Iridium Alloy Ingot
Combine Advanced Alloy Plates, Carbon Plates, Iridium Ingots, and a Diamond to form the uncompressed Iridium Alloy Ingot.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/iridium_alloy_ingot" />

## 2. Iridium Alloy Plate
The raw ingot must be compressed using an Implosion Compressor and ITNT to form the usable Iridium Alloy Plate.
