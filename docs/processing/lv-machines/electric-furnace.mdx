## Overview
The Electric Furnace functions exactly like a vanilla furnace but operates faster and uses LV power instead of solid fuel.

## Behavior
- **Input:** 1 Item Slot
- **Output:** 1 Item Slot
- **Power:** 32 E/t (from Low Voltage)

## Recipes accepted
*The Electric Furnace accepts all standard Minecraft smelting recipes.*
