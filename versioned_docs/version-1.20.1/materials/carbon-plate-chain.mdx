# Carbon Plate Chain

The Carbon Plate is an essential intermediate component for advanced machines, solar panels, and nano armor. It requires a specific processing chain to craft.

## 1. Raw Carbon Fiber
First, you must process Coal into Coal Dust using a Grinder or Industrial Grinder. Then, craft 4 Coal Dust into Raw Carbon Fiber.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/raw_carbon_fiber" />

## 2. Raw Carbon Mesh
Next, combine two Raw Carbon Fibers together to create a Raw Carbon Mesh.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/raw_carbon_mesh" />

## 3. Carbon Plate
Finally, compress the Raw Carbon Mesh in a Compressor to yield a finished Carbon Plate.
