# Getting Started with Tech Reborn

This guide will walk you through the very basics of Tech Reborn, up to your first automated Low Voltage (LV) setup.

## 1. Rubber & Treetaps

Your first goal is to gather Rubber, which is essential for insulating cables.
Find a Rubber Tree in the world. Craft a Treetap and use it on the flowing sap spots on the tree to harvest Sap. 

*(See <!-- TODO link: mechanics/cables-and-transformers -->Cables & Transformers<!-- /TODO --> for more on Rubber).*

## 2. Refined Iron

You will need Refined Iron to craft basic machine components. You can obtain Refined Iron by smelting standard Iron Ingots in a vanilla Furnace.

## 5. Your First Machines

With power generated, you can connect machines using Insulated Copper Cables. Here are three essential machines to start with:

### Electric Furnace
A faster, more efficient furnace that runs on electricity instead of solid fuel.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/electric_furnace" />

### Extractor
The Extractor is highly recommended early on because it processes Sap into 3 Rubber instead of the 1 Rubber you get from smelting Sap in a furnace.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/extractor" />

### Compressor & Grinder
The Compressor turns ingots into Plates, and the Grinder turns ingots/ores into Dusts. Both are vital for progressing to Medium Voltage (MV).

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/compressor" />
<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/grinder" />

## 6. Alloy Smelter

Finally, build an Alloy Smelter. This machine combines multiple materials into alloys like Bronze, which you will need to upgrade to the next tier of machines.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/alloy_smelter" />

## What's Next?

Congratulations, you now have basic LV automation! Your next goals should be:
- Scaling up your power generation.
- Upgrading to MV machines.
- Building your first Industrial Blast Furnace (IBF) to access Steel and Aluminum.
