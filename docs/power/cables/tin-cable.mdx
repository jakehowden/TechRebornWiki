## Overview
The Tin Cable is a basic cable for transferring Low Voltage (LV) power. It does not have an insulated variant.

## Behavior
- **Tier:** Low (LV)
- **Transfer Rate:** up to 32 E/t
- **Insulated:** No
- **Electrocution Hazard:** Yes (if enabled in `techreborn.cfg`)
