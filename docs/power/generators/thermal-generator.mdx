## Overview
The Thermal Generator provides energy when placed adjacent to lava source blocks or when lava is pumped directly into it.

<RecipeFromData id="techreborn:crafting/thermal_generator" />

## Behavior
- Input: Lava (adjacent blocks or piped fluid).
- Output: Energy (E).
- Configurable values: `thermalGeneratorMaxOutput`, `thermalGeneratorMaxEnergy`, `thermalGeneratorEnergyPerTick`
