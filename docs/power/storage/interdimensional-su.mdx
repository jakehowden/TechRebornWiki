## Overview
The Interdimensional SU (IDSU) is an end-game energy storage unit. All IDSUs placed by the same player share the exact same energy buffer, regardless of what dimension they are in.

## Behavior
- Input: Insane Voltage (IV).

- Special: Power is shared across all your IDSUs globally.
